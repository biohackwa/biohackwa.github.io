Welcome Biohackers!

"BioHack Academy 4 is once again going to rock the BioHack planet! Spread across four different continents you are going to build, use and share new biohack labs and practice how to hack biotechnology."-- Waag Society

At UWA, we will join the BioHack Academy for the first time. We will tune into live-streamed lectures every week, and each week we will build one bio lab tool and try it out on some biomaterials. Short practicals will help you along the way to learn the necessary skills, but it will be up to you to explore them further and pursue your own personal goals. Toward the end of the course, you will start to drive your own ideas and projects.

Check out the Course Structure here!

Coordination

This page (http://biohackwa.github.io/) will be used for local (UWA) content, news, communication and planning. Please keep up to date with each website. A daily schedule will be released shortly.
